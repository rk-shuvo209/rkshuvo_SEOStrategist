I start with a full technical crawl using Screaming Frog to map crawl errors, broken links, duplicate content, and structural issues. I then cross-reference Core Web Vitals in PageSpeed Insights and pull impression and click data from Google Search Console to identify pages with strong impressions but weak CTR. The audit ends with a prioritised action list sorted by potential impact, not just effort.

Technical fixes and on-page optimisations often produce visible improvements within 4 to 8 weeks as search engines recrawl updated pages. Sustainable organic traffic growth typically becomes clear within 3 to 6 months, depending on your domain's existing authority and competition in your niche. I set honest timelines at the start of every project rather than making promises that rely on luck.
